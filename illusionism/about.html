Illusionism

About

Hello! My name is Kyle Kutner. I am a Graphic Design Senior who paints and draws. I took Illusionism in the Fall semester of my Junior year and have been employing illusionistic techniques in my paintings ever since.

Illusionism is defined as a technique of using highly illusory effects in art or decoration in order to deceive the human eye. These methods and the use of perspective create an impression of three-dimensional reality.

Though this is a demanding course, it will undoubtedly make you a stronger artist. Please come to class mentally and physically prepared.
