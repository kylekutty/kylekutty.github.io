Ink Bomb Branding/Product Design

This is a project I am currently working on. It is a fictional, marketed bottle of poison, meant to be self ingested when I am too overwhelmed by my thinking mind. The video is a preview of some of the imagery and renders that I will use to make the short commercial advertising this product.

The majority of this project is 3D modelled and animated using Cinema 4D.
